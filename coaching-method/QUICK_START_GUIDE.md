# Quick Start Guide

This reflective coaching technique can be used with conversational AI tools such as ChatGPT.

The process is simple and requires only a few steps.

## Step 1: Open a Conversation

Open a new conversation with ChatGPT or another conversational AI system.

## Step 2: Paste the Coaching Prompt

Copy the operational prompt provided with this guide and paste it as your first message in the conversation.

This prompt configures the AI to operate as a reflective coach following the method described in this guide.

## Step 3: Begin the Conversation

Once the prompt has been entered, begin the conversation by describing the situation you would like to explore.

You do not need to provide a perfectly structured explanation. A short description of your situation or curiosity is enough.

Examples might include:

- “I feel like my imagination has become dormant, and I’d like to rediscover it while thinking about a possible career pivot.”
- “I’ve been leading a team through several difficult decisions and want to reflect more deeply on my leadership approach.”
- “There’s a recurring problem in my work that I don’t think I’m seeing clearly yet.”
- “I want to explore a question that keeps coming up in my professional life.”

The AI will respond by asking clarifying questions and gradually building a reflective plan with you.

## Step 4: Follow the Plan

Once a plan is proposed and accepted, the conversation will guide you through short reflection sessions and occasional synthesis exercises.

These sessions are typically brief, allowing you to explore ideas without overwhelming your schedule.

## Step 5: Reflect and Continue

As the process unfolds, you may begin noticing patterns or insights that were previously difficult to see.

At the end of a cycle, you can either pause or begin a new round of reflection.

The technique works best when treated as an ongoing habit rather than a one-time exercise.

# Saving and Resuming Progress

AI chat sessions do not always retain memory across long conversations.

This method uses a simple file called:

REFLECTION_STATE.md

Think of this as a **save file for your reflection process**.

---

## Create a Checkpoint

At any time you can ask the AI coach:

Create a checkpoint.

The AI will generate an updated reflection state document.

Copy and save this file somewhere safe
(Notes app, document folder, etc.).

---

## Resume Later

To continue your reflection in a new chat session:

1. Paste the entire Reflection State file into the conversation.
2. Ask the AI coach:

Resume from this checkpoint.

The AI will read the file and continue the reflective coaching process from the correct phase.
